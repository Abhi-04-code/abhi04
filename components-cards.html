BOOTSTRAP CARDS

▶ Card Overview

Bootstrap cards are flexible and extensible content containers with multiple variants and options. They provide a clean and organized way to display various types of content including images, text, lists, and buttons.

Pro Tip: Cards are perfect for creating modern, responsive layouts for content display, product showcases, user profiles, and dashboard widgets.

▶ Card Options

Card Classes and Components

Class/Component Description Usage
.card Base card container Required for all cards
.card-body Main content area Contains card content
.card-header Card header section Optional header content
.card-footer Card footer section Optional footer content
.card-img-top Top image placement Image at top of card
.card-img-overlay Content over image Text overlay on card image
.card-group Card group container Groups cards with equal width

Card Best Practices

  • Consistent Design: Maintain consistent spacing, typography, and colors across all cards.
  • Clear Hierarchy: Use proper heading levels and visual hierarchy to guide users.
  • Visual Balance: Ensure images, text, and actions are well-balanced within the card.
  • Performance: Optimize images and content for fast loading times.
