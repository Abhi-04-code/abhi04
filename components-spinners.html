BOOTSTRAP SPINNERS

▶ Spinners Overview

Bootstrap spinners indicate loading states for components or pages. They're built entirely with HTML, CSS, and no JavaScript, making them lightweight and easy to customize.

Pro Tip: Spinners can be used inside buttons, as standalone elements, or with text to provide context about what's loading.

Spinner Best Practices

  • Accessibility: Always include role="status" and visually-hidden text for screen readers.
  • Contextual Colors: Use appropriate colors that match your application's status indicators.
  • Appropriate Duration: Use spinners for operations that take 1-10 seconds; consider progress bars for longer tasks.
  • Clear Messaging: Provide text labels to explain what is being loaded or processed.
  • Proper Placement: Position spinners where they won't disrupt the user's workflow.
